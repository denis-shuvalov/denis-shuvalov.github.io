How It Works
1
Draw a Token

Randomly pair with another player — no pressure or awkward choosing.

2
Set the Tone

Choose a deck that matches your comfort level with this particular person.

3
Pick a Card

Follow the prompt, skip it, or adapt it — you’re always free to choose.

4
Connect

Discover, feel, express, engage, or simply be — let the moment unfold.

It’s not about going further — it’s about going slower, together.

About the Creator

Hi, I’m Angelina — a UX designer passionate about human connection, consent, and exploring boundaries with curiosity and care.

I created ConSense after falling in love with conscious touch workshops. I wanted to bring the beauty of consent-based connection to people who’ve never experienced spaces like that — through something playful, simple, and safe.

As a neurodivergent person, I know how hard it can be to navigate social cues and expectations. This game is my way of offering a softer path — where communication is clear, choice is always yours, and closeness never comes with pressure.

 “Pressure doesn’t bring us closer. Honesty does.”
Thank you for supporting this project — I hope ConSense brings you as much warmth and discovery as it brought me while creating it.
